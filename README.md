**Project Overview**
--------
Travel on a Budget is a smart travel planning application that prioritizes financial clarity from the start. The platform structures personalized trips inside and outside Egypt based on a predefined budget, accommodation level, and trip duration, ensuring that every recommendation remains realistic and achievable. By integrating estimated costs for transportation, lodging, food, and activities into one organized plan, the app transforms budget limitations into a clear, structured, and stress-free travel experience.
---
**Project Objectives**
---------
- Structure trip recommendations based on a predefined budget to ensure realistic and achievable plans.
- Simplify the travel planning process by consolidating cost estimates and destination information into one system.
- Reduce financial stress and uncertainty associated with trip organization.
- Provide personalized recommendations aligned with user preferences and spending limits.
- Promote financial awareness by turning budget constraints into a strategic planning advantage
---
**Project Scope**
----------
- Destinations inside and outside Egypt.
- Trip generation based on budget, duration, and accommodation level.
- Estimated cost breakdown for transportation, lodging, food, and activities.
- Interactive map and AI-assisted support.
